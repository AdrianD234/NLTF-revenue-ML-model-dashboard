# VISUAL_TARGET_SPEC.lock.md

The four supplied images are the visual target for this sprint. They are layout and style references, not data sources.

## Locked Visual Direction

- Waka Kotahi-inspired management dashboard.
- White background, deep navy, lime green, teal, subtle grey borders and compact rounded cards.
- Four primary top navigation labels only.
- Compact governance filters above the KPI row.
- Executive-ready chart hierarchy with minimal prose.
- No giant decorative circles or ellipses around the candidate frontier.
- No raw internal field names in hovers or labels.
- No chart card may be blank when data exists.
- Missing secondary data must be shown as a compact unavailable state.

## Page Layout Target

- Overview: two-panel upper chart row, two-panel lower chart row.
- Diagnostics: two-by-two diagnostic evidence grid.
- Scenario Comparison: comparison controls, KPI row, then two-by-two comparison grid.
- Schiff Benchmark: KPI row, then two-by-two benchmark grid.
