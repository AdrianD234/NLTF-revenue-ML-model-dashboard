# PRODUCT_HARDENING_SPRINT.lock.md

## Purpose

The dashboard must be improved beyond basic functionality into a presentation-ready product.

## Mandatory sprint quota

After the first passing verification, the agent must complete:

- at least 20 product-hardening loops;
- at least 15 material product improvements;
- at least 25 new or strengthened tests/assertions;
- at least 5 reviewer reports;
- at least 8 final screenshots;
- at least 1 final management-readiness report.

## Loop structure

Each loop must:
1. identify the weakest page/feature;
2. implement a material improvement;
3. strengthen tests or browser assertions;
4. rerun verification;
5. inspect the page in the browser;
6. save a screenshot;
7. update all artifacts.

## Minimum score

Each page must score at least:

- 9.5/10 deep quality;
- 9/10 visual reference similarity;
- 9/10 interaction quality;
- 9/10 management usefulness.

## Stop rule

Do not stop until the sprint quota is met and every score threshold is passed.

If the session cannot continue, write `.agent_state.md` and stop as in-progress.
